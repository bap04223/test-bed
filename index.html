‚ùì How to Use the Training System

üöÄ Getting Started

  1. Load Configurations: Enter your Chatbot ID and Scenario Sets ID, then click the load buttons to populate the dropdown menus.
  2. Select Chatbot: Choose the AI chatbot configuration you want to practice with from the dropdown.
  3. Select Scenario Set: Choose the collection of patient scenarios you want to use.
  4. Choose Sheet: Select the specific sheet containing your scenarios.
  5. Pick a Scenario: Either select a specific patient scenario or use "Random Scenario" for surprise practice.

üí¨ During the Consultation

  1. Start Conversing: Type your opening message in the text box and click "Send Message" or press Enter.
  2. Professional Communication: Communicate as you would with a real patient - introduce yourself, ask appropriate questions, provide advice.
  3. View Patient Image: If available, click the patient image button to see visual information about your patient.
  4. Continue Dialogue: The AI patient will respond realistically - continue the consultation until you've addressed their needs.

üìä Managing Your Session

  • Clear Chat: Remove all messages to start fresh with the same scenario
  • New Session: Start completely over with a new session ID
  • Export Chat: Download your conversation as a text file for review
  • Submit for Evaluation: Send your consultation to your instructor for feedback

üéØ Best Practices

  • Be Professional: Practice real-world communication skills
  • Ask Questions: Gather information about symptoms, medications, allergies
  • Provide Clear Advice: Give specific, actionable recommendations
  • Show Empathy: Acknowledge patient concerns and feelings
  • Follow Up: Ask if the patient has any other questions

üîß Troubleshooting

  • No Response from AI: Check that you've selected a chatbot configuration and scenario
  • Can't Load Scenarios: Verify your Google Sheet IDs are correct and sheets are publicly accessible
  • Image Won't Load: Ensure the image URL is set to "Anyone with link can view"
  • Submission Failed: Check your internet connection and try again

üí° Need More Help?

If you encounter issues or have suggestions for improvement, click the Feedback button above to submit a report. Include details about what you were trying to do and what went wrong.
