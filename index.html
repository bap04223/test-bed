Pharmacy Consultation Training

Practice your consultation skills with an AI-powered patient simulator

📋 Instructions

  1. Enter your n8n webhook URLs in the Configuration section
  2. Load your Google Sheet with patient scenarios
  3. Select a patient scenario from the dropdown (or choose random)
  1. Initialize your D-ID avatar with your API key
  2. Hold down the "Push to Talk" button and speak your consultation
  3. The AI patient will respond with both text and avatar video
  1. Continue the conversation by speaking your responses
  2. Use "Submit Conversation" to get evaluation feedback
  3. Refresh the browser after each scenario for a new session

Tips: Speak clearly, ask open-ended questions, and practice active listening skills!
